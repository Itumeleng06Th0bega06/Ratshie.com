About Us

Who We Are

Ratshie (Pty) Ltd is a registered private company based in Seoding Village, Kuruman, Northern Cape. We operate a dual model automotive service: a car repairs workshop and a mobile car spares delivery business. Our aim is to bridge the gap in rural vehicle care and parts supply with accessible, affordable, and high-quality services.
